About

Here at King’s we like to do things a little differently. As a break from the long-standing tradition of May Balls, King’s invites you to shed the ties and ball gowns and enjoy a night of fancy dress on the transformed grounds of our iconic college. As the only fancy dress event in May Week we implore you to grab the glue guns and sequins and join 1,500 other guests in an unparalleled night of fun.

This year’s King’s Affair will be taking place on Wednesday the 20th of June and features a number of stages for live acts, DJs and talented musicians with performances to suit any taste. Expect acts that will enthral and entertain, with no shortage of delectable treats and refreshments to keep you dancing all night.

We invite you to this year’s King’s Affair, take our hand and let us lead you into the Music of Spheres...
