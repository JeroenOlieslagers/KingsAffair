Ticketing & Identification

Tickets have been issued electronically in the format of a QR Code, which may be checked at the following link: https://webapp.getqpay.com

On the day of the event, please have a copy of the QR Code prepared on your smart phone or a printed copy. These codes will be scanned in order to permit entry into the event. Please note that physical tickets will not be distributed.

We request that guests bring forms of identification other than their college card, such as their driving license or passport which matches the name on their ticket. A failure to produce an acceptable form of identification may result in the refusal of entry. Under 18s will be not be permitted entry to the event.

Guests will be given wristbands on arrival. We ask that guests cooperate with security who may at any point in the evening request that these wristbands be presented. Those found without wristbands will be escorted out of the event.

Queueing

General entry guests may queue on Trinity Lane and will enter through the North Door of the Chapel, with doors opening at 9pm. Guests may begin queuing from 7:45.

Guests with queue jump may enter through the main gate on King's Parade, and may begin queuing from 8pm.

Last entry to the event is at 10:30.

No Re-Entry

Re-entry to the King’s Affair is strictly prohibited. Those electing to leave the event before 5am will have their wristbands removed and may not re-enter the event.

Drugs, Alcohol and Behaviour Policy

The Committee and College has a zero tolerance policy for illegal substances. Those found in possession of illegal substances will be evicted from the site and reported to the police.

Guests who are noticeably inebriated will not be served at the bars. The committee also reserves the right to serve single, rather than double drinks. The committee reserves the right to refuse entry to guests who are noticeably intoxicated and to remove guests who act in a disruptive, aggressive or otherwise anti-social manner. The police, as well as the student’s Dean may be contacted if appropriate.

Disability Access

Further, those with disabilities (who have not already) are requested to get in contact with the committee to ensure we are able to take all precautions necessary and guarantee access. Please email chair@kingsaffair.com

No Cloakroom

Please note there will be no cloakroom at the event.

In case of any questions, please do not hesitate to get in contact with the committee. We look forward to welcoming you to the Music of the Spheres!
